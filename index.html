Hello, world!

Hi! I'm Kayalash. I'm a first year computer science student at the University of Waterloo. I currently live in Waterloo but my home is in Toronto.

I am passionate about using my skills to create things that help average people and fix things that are otherwise overlooked. I was fortunate enough to grow up in a period of incredible progress, both socially and technologically. I have benefited from the remarkable work of the extraordinary people that have come before me, and I hope that one day I will make/do something that has even a fraction of the impact that these people have had on me. Some of my most important inspirations include:

  • Bill Gates
  • Mark Zuckerberg
  • Hillary Clinton
  • Joe Biden
  • Barack Obama
  • Melinda Gates
  • Sergey Brin
  • Larry Page
  • Leonardo Dicaprio
  • Jon Stewart
  • Stephen Colbert
  • Justin Trudeau
  • George Clooney
  • Karlie Kloss

As you can see from the list above, I have a keen interest in politics, dating back to Obama's re-election in 2012 and the first time I watched The Daily Show. I admire politicians who put all their energy and time into doing what they believe to be right andfighting against injustice, all the while putting their lives on hold to serve the people. I always try to make an effort to see every side of an issue. I believe that bringing people together based on the things we share in common is much better than dividing us apart based on our political views. I hope that one day I will have the chance to work for the public sector in some way or another.

Apart from politics, I am a binge-watcher of Movies and especially TV shows. I read a few Books, but I really enjoy reading Articles on the internet about a wide variety of issues.

I love to learn and I'm always looking for new things to experience. Thanks for stopping by and feel free to Contact me.
